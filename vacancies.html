Job Description

We are hiring Area Sales Executives to join our fast-growing platform. Ideal candidates will be energetic, self-driven, and passionate about working in the field to connect local shops with our online marketplace.


Key Responsibilities:
  • Visit and onboard shops onto the platform.
  • Help shop owners list products, update prices, and maintain their online presence.
  • Distribute promotional cards.
  • Support shop owners with delivery coordination and product-related assistance.
  • Continuously bring new shops and promote the platform in the assigned area.
  • Provide customer service and build strong shop relationships.
  • Participate in virtual deliveries or coordinate delivery help when needed.
Requirements:
  • Minimum 1 year of experience in field sales or marketing in UAE/in country.
  • Fluent in English is a must.
  • Hardworking, presentable, and good communication skills.
  • Ability to work independently and meet performance targets.
Nationalities: Any

Driving License: Preferred, but not mandatory
