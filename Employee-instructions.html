Onboarding Instructions – Marketing & Delivery Associate

Accommodation & Equipment:

  • Bedspace provided at work location.
  • Device given for adding stores, uploading products, receiving orders, and handling cash payments.
  • Purchase a SIM card for business use only (we top up data).

Work Hours:

  • 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM in assigned area.

Step 1 – Initial Shop Listing (First 5 Days)

  • Add all shops in your delivery area to the system.
  • Include a clear shopfront picture and the shop name.
  • These will be shown as “Not Partnered.”
  • Partner with at least one store in each category:
    • Grocery
    • Cafeteria
    • Restaurant
    • Electronics
    • Fashion
  • Complete within 5 days.

Step 2 – Promotion (From Day 6 Onwards)

  • Distribute promotional cards:
    • Morning: 9:00 AM
    • Evening: 7:00 PM
  • Repeat daily until orders come regularly from same buildings.
  • Place cards in receptions, door-to-door, and on cars.

Step 3 – Order Handling & Delivery Support

  • If orders become too many to handle, an extra delivery person will be assigned.

Step 4 – Continuous Shop Partnerships

  • When free, partner with more shops to expand coverage.

Partnering with Stores

  • Ask the shop for permission to take pictures.
  • If they ask why, tell them: “We will sell your products in our app. Your sales will increase.”
  • Take a 1:1 photo of the shop from outside.
  • Create a new business email for the shop:
    eg:shopname.nearbys@gmail.com
  • Go to https://nearbys.online/pages/join and fill the details, then submit.
  • Open https://nearbys.sp-seller.webkul.com/index.php
    (Use Dual Space app to create a new account).
  • Click Join, then fill:
    • Seller Name: Shop name (max 15 characters)
    • Email
    • Password: 111111
    Click Create Account.
  • Add products and remember:
    • Tags
    • Margins: 10% for grocery, 20% for others
